Riset: LLM (GPT, Gemini) untuk Trading

Studi University of Florida:

ChatGPT bisa prediksi daily stock returns dengan akurasi tinggi!

Return: +400% cumulative return (Oct 2021 - Dec 2022)

Bagaimana LLM Bekerja:

  • Sentiment Analysis - baca news, social media
  • Pattern Recognition - dari text, bukan chart
  • Generate Trading Signals - "alphas"
  • Combine dengan Traditional Models

Kelemahannya:

  • Overly conservative di bull market
  • Overly aggressive di bear market
  • Butuh fine-tuning untuk domain finance
  • Tidak real-time (latency)

Verdict: LLM BISA predict, tapi bukan dari candlestick!

Bagaimana Polymarket Bot Menang:

  • Arbitrage - beli YES + NO ketika total < $1
  • Cross-platform arbitrage - Polymarket vs Kalshi vs PredictIt
  • Mispricing detection - harga odds yang salah
  • High-frequency trading - eksekusi dalam milidetik
  • Systematic NO farming - 70%+ markets resolve to NO

Perbedaan Kunci: Polymarket vs Real Market

Polymarket Forex/Stock
Binary outcome (YES/NO) Continuous price movement
Fixed resolution time No fixed end
Arbitrage opportunities exist Arbitrage = very rare
Easier to predict Much harder

Kenapa Polymarket AI Berhasil, Forex AI Gagal?

Polymarket Advantages:

  • Defined outcome - Will Trump win? YES/NO
  • Information edge - siapa dapat news duluan menang
  • Arbitrage real - price mismatch exploitable
  • Less random - based on real events
  • Market inefficiency - masih baru

Forex/Stock Challenges:

  • Continuous price - infinite possibilities
  • No clear outcome - kapan "selesai"?
  • High competition - trillion dollar industry
  • More random - noise > signal
  • Market very efficient - hard to beat

Polymarket = Lebih Prediktabel karena Binary dan Event-Based

Forex = Random walk dengan sedikit trend, jauh lebih sulit

Neural Network vs Random Forest

Aspect Random Forest Neural Network
Accuracy 85-90% direction Slightly higher in complex data
Speed Faster training Slower, needs GPU
Overfitting More robust Prone to overfit
Interpretability Feature importance Black box
Data needed Works with less data Needs more data
Best for Tabular, structured Complex, non-linear

Verdict untuk Trading:

Random Forest seringkali lebih baik untuk trading karena:

  • Data trading = structured/tabular
  • Overfitting = musuh utama di trading
  • Interpretability penting untuk refinement

Neural Network bagus jika: data sangat besar, complex patterns, combined dengan LLM

Low Win Rate + High RR = Valid Strategy!

20% Win Rate + 1:5 RR = PROFITABLE
Expectancy = (Win% x Avg Win) - (Loss% x Avg Loss)

20% WR, 1:5 RR:
= (0.20 x 5) - (0.80 x 1)
= 1.0 - 0.8
= +0.2R per trade

100 trades x 0.2R = 20R profit!

Strategi Kita (Strategy 26):

Win Rate 19.7%
RR 1:5
Expectancy +0.185R/trade
Result +182% return

Ini BUKAN Random!

Jika random (50% WR dengan 1:5 RR):

= (0.50 x 5) - (0.50 x 1) = +2R/trade

TAPI kita tidak dapat 50% WR karena:

  • Stop loss lebih dekat dari TP
  • Market noise hits SL more often

Pattern Recognition di Market: Realita

Candlestick DOES represent market dynamics, BUT...

  • Patterns exist - tapi weak (50-60% edge at best)
  • Context matters MORE than individual pattern
  • Self-fulfilling - banyak trader lihat = pattern works
  • Self-defeating - terlalu banyak uses = stops working

Apa yang BISA diprediksi:

  • Market regime (trend/range)
  • Volatility (akan besar/kecil)
  • Support/resistance reaction
  • Zone respect probability

Apa yang TIDAK BISA:

  • Exact price in 1 hour
  • 100% win rate
  • Perfect timing
  • News impact

Edge kecil + risk management baik + banyak trades = PROFITABLE

MQL5 EA: Bullshit atau Real?

Yang BULLSHIT:

  • "99% win rate" claims
  • "$100 to $1M in 3 months"
  • Overfit backtests
  • Martingale hidden inside
  • Curve-fitted parameters
  • No real forward test

Yang BISA Real:

  • Consistent 10-30% yearly
  • Controlled drawdown
  • Clear logic/strategy
  • Forward tested results
  • Risk management built-in
  • Not hiding Martingale

Red Flags:

  • Screenshot only (no myfxbook link)
  • Too good to be true results
  • No source code inspection
  • "Secret algorithm"
  • Selling for $50-500 (if it works, why sell?)

Apa yang Bisa Diperbaiki?
